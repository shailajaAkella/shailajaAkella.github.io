About me

I am a Scientist II at the Allen Institute in Seattle, WA, where I develop machine learning and dynamical systems methods to understand brain-wide computations during flexible behavior. My work focuses on neural dynamics modeling, motor-signal decoding, and large-scale analysis of electrophysiology and behavior.

I have 7+ years of research experience spanning recurrent neural networks, state-space methods, GLM-HMMs, sparse coding frameworks, and information-theoretic analysis of brain–behavior interactions.
